Is the climate/operations decomposition sound on tier results?Draft for team review

Evaluation of the climate-vs-operations split shown on tier results — the interpretive core of the public website. Verdict: sound, with conditions. The operations effect is a property of a strategy–climate pair, not of a strategy alone; the document enumerates seven ways readers could be misled, the guardrail for each, and six empirical tests (T1–T6) to run as a gate before decomposition figures ship. Companion decision: the split is never applied to raw model output (core concept 17).
