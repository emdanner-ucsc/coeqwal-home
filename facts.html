COEQWAL canonical facts ledger

Every load-bearing fact, its authority, and where it is echoed — so drift gets caught mechanically. First compiled 2026-07-11. Back to working pages.

Pages don't go stale on their own; facts move and their copies don't. This ledger lists each fact that appears in more than one place, the single authority that owns it, and the surfaces that echo it. To audit: check each echo against the authority column. When a fact changes, update the authority first, then walk its echo list.

Quarantine rule. The synthetic prototypes (the v0.x briefs, the demos, the gallery figures) deliberately carry prototype-era numbers — 24 strategies × 4 climates, 96 scenarios, an editorial "HC3", s0036 as a served strategy. Their synthetic banner is the quarantine boundary: facts inside it are not enforced. Enforced surfaces are the hub pages, the brief tracker, the real-data (dev) briefs, the water map, and anything headed to the public site.
