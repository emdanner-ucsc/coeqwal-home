What is a brief?

A brief is a short, citable document. Briefs on this page fall into two classes.

Results briefs report modeled tier results from a fixed set of simulations — for a theme, a sector, or a place. They report what the model produces; they do not predict what will happen, judge whether results are acceptable, or recommend any course of action.

Reference briefs describe the pieces the simulations are built from — what a strategy changes about system operations, or what a hydroclimate model assumes. They are part of the COEQWAL process and contain no model results.
