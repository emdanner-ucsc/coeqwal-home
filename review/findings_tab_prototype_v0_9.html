Orientation

The 24 strategies at a glance

COEQWAL models 24 management strategies, each simulated once under each of five hydroclimates. A scenario is one strategy combined with one hydroclimate: 24 strategies × 5 hydroclimates gives 120 scenarios, of which 115 are currently in the served library. Each code in the table identifies one scenario; hydroclimate columns run from historical conditions to extreme climate stress.

What is a brief?

A brief is a short, citable document reporting modeled results from a fixed set of simulations. Briefs report what the model produces; they do not predict what will happen, judge whether results are acceptable, or recommend any course of action.
