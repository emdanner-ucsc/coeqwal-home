Snapshots of the visualization work in progress — figures and interactive tools built around the COEQWAL scenario results. Some are candidates for the public website; others are working instruments or explorations kept for reference.

All figures use synthetic rehearsal data — placeholder values with the right shape, not COEQWAL results. Snapshots taken 2026-06-04.

Climate vs operations decomposition

Guided stepper

The chosen direction for the public website. A guided three-step decomposition — today, then climate, then operations — in continuous tier space, one step per click.

Scenario columns by climate

Four climate columns with all 24 strategies evenly slotted in each; vertical position is the continuous tier value. Hovering splits a dot into baseline + climate + operations; clicking pins it. Used as Figure 2 in the location brief prototype.

The Landscape and related views

Landscape explorer (analyst tool)

The interactive landscape explorer: scenario performance against the baseline diagonal by sector, with scenario, climate-future, equity, overlay, and grouping controls.

Landscape figure

A fixed view of the landscape: one strategy (salmon-friendly flows) across all climates, sector means against the baseline diagonal.

Quadrant plot

Climate effect on one axis, strategy effect on the other, so each sector or location lands in one of four stories: climate worsens or improves, crossed with strategy worsens or improves.
