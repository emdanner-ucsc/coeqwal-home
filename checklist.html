TeamHydroclimate vocabulary: harmonize or document the two public naming systems

Likewise the map's scenario labels are run-listing descriptions rather than the canonical short titles, and its s0020 label says "Baseline" where the canon says "current operations". Decide: adopt one vocabulary, or document the mapping visibly on both surfaces.
