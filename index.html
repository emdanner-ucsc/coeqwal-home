COEQWAL working pages

Published working material from Eric Danner's COEQWAL projects. Page published 2026-07-19 11:15 AM.

COEQWAL — the Collaboratory for Equity in Water Allocation — studies how alternative water management strategies and projected climates change outcomes for California's water system, and communicates the results to the public. This page collects the working pages I publish for the team: trackers, figures, and prototypes. Everything here is working material, not the public COEQWAL website.
